Samurai Chopper

SSamurai Chopper is a fast-paced, addictive game that tests your reflexes. Play as a skilled samurai chopping wood by tapping the screen while avoiding randomly appearing branches. The game starts simple but becomes more challenging as branches appear more frequently and quickly, requiring precise and quick reactions. Featuring vibrant Japanese-themed visuals and smooth animations, Samurai Chopper offers a fun and engaging experience. Compete on leaderboards, collect coins, and unlock various samurai costumes and skins to customize your character. Samurai Chopper is coming soon to Google Play. Get ready for a new adventure!
